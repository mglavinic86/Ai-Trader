# Skill: SMC Trading (Smart Money Concepts)

> Koristi ovaj skill za institucionalni price action pristup.

## Kada aktivirati

Aktiviraj kada korisnik kaze:
- "SMC analiza"
- "smart money"
- "institutional analysis"
- "order flow analiza"
- "IOF"

## Timeframe

- **Bias:** Daily, H4
- **Zone Identification:** H1, H4
- **Entry:** M15, M5

## Workflow

```
1. HTF Bias (Daily/H4 candle direction)
   |
2. Identify Zone of Interest (FVG, OB, Liquidity)
   |
3. Wait for Price to Enter Zone
   |
4. LTF Confirmation (CISD, BOS, iFVG)
   |
5. Entry with Proper Risk Management
```

## Entry pravila

1. **Odredi HTF Bias**
   - Provjeri Daily/H4 svijecu
   - Bullish = trazi LONG, Bearish = trazi SHORT

2. **Identificiraj Zonu**
   - Fair Value Gap (FVG)
   - Order Block (OB)
   - Liquidity level (PDH/PDL, PWH/PWL)

3. **Cekaj Potvrdu na LTF**
   - CISD (Change in State of Delivery)
   - Novi FVG u smjeru biasa
   - iFVG formacija

4. **Entry**
   - Na FVG, OB ili iFVG
   - SL ispod/iznad zone
   - Target: Sljedeca liquidity razina

## Exit pravila

- **Stop Loss:** Ispod/iznad zone + buffer
- **Take Profit:** Sljedeca liquidity (PDH/PDL ili PWH/PWL)
- **R:R Minimum:** 1:2
- **Partial TP:** 50% na 1:1, ostatak na 1:2+

## Key Concepts

| Concept | Koristi za |
|---------|-----------|
| FVG | Entry zone |
| OB | Entry zone |
| Liquidity | Targets |
| BOS | Trend continuation |
| CISD | Trend reversal |
| iFVG | Confirmation |

## IZBJEGAVAJ

- Trading protiv HTF biasa
- Ulaz bez CISD/confirmation na LTF
- Prvih 30 min svake sesije
- Za vrijeme major news
- Nizak volume periodi

## Risk Management

- Max 1% per trade
- Max 2 aktivna SMC tradea
- Stop nakon 2 consecutive gubitka
