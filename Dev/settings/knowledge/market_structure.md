# Market Structure Knowledge Base

> Temelj za razumijevanje kretanja cijene kroz multi-timeframe analizu.

## 1. Core Concept: One Candle = One Structure

**Fundamental Principle:**
- Jedna svijeca na visem timeframe-u sadrzi cijelu strukturu na nizem timeframe-u
- Kada "zoomiras" jednu svijecu, vidis kompletan trend koji ju je formirao

### Candle-to-Structure Mapping

| HTF Candle Type | LTF Structure |
|-----------------|---------------|
| **Bullish candle** | Bullish trend (HH/HL) na LTF |
| **Bearish candle** | Bearish trend (LH/LL) na LTF |

**Key Insight:** Ako daily svijeca zatvori bullish, na H1 timeframe-u postoji bullish trend.

---

## 2. Timeframe Alignment Rules

### Standard Timeframe Correlation

| Higher Timeframe (HTF) | Lower Timeframe (LTF) Structure |
|------------------------|--------------------------------|
| **Weekly (W1)** | H4 structure |
| **Daily (D1)** | H1 structure |
| **H4** | M15 structure |
| **H1** | M5 structure |

### How to Apply

1. **Identify HTF candle direction** (bullish/bearish)
2. **Drop to corresponding LTF** to see the internal structure
3. **Trade in alignment** with the HTF candle direction

---

## 3. Trend Identification

### Bullish Trend (Uptrend)
- **Pattern:** Higher Highs (HH) and Higher Lows (HL)
- **Indicates:** Buyers have control

### Bearish Trend (Downtrend)
- **Pattern:** Lower Highs (LH) and Lower Lows (LL)
- **Indicates:** Sellers have control

---

## 4. Continuation vs Reversal Candles

### Continuation Candle

**Definition:** Svijeca koja nastavlja u istom smjeru kao prethodni trend.

**Trading Implication:**
- Bullish continuation = LTF bullish trend continues
- **Action:** Look for LONG entries on pullbacks to zones (FVG, Liquidity, OB)

### Reversal Candle

**Definition:** Svijeca koja zatvara suprotno od prethodnog trenda.

**Trading Implication:**
- Bearish reversal after bullish trend = LTF will shift from bullish to bearish
- **Action:** Look for SHORT entries after structure shift confirms

---

## 5. Trading Direction (Bias) Determination

### The Bias Filter

HTF svijeca sluzi kao **directional filter** za LTF trades.

| HTF Candle | Bias | LTF Action |
|------------|------|------------|
| Bullish continuation | **LONG** | Look for buy setups on LTF |
| Bearish continuation | **SHORT** | Look for sell setups on LTF |
| Bullish reversal (after downtrend) | **LONG** | Wait for LTF bullish BOS, then buy |
| Bearish reversal (after uptrend) | **SHORT** | Wait for LTF bearish BOS, then sell |

### Key Rule
> **Uvijek trguj u smjeru prethodne svijece - bilo reversal ili continuation.**

---

## 6. Multi-Timeframe Analysis Workflow

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Odredi HTF Direction (Daily / H4)**
   - Pogledaj zadnju zatvorenu svijecu
   - Identificiraj je li bullish ili bearish
   - Odredi je li continuation ili reversal

2. **Understand the Implication**
   - Continuation bullish: H1 ima bullish strukturu, trazi longove
   - Continuation bearish: H1 ima bearish strukturu, trazi shortove
   - Reversal: Cekaj potvrdu na LTF

3. **Find Entry Zone on LTF**
   - FVG (Fair Value Gap)
   - Liquidity ($$)
   - OB (Order Block)

4. **Execute Trade**
   - Entry at zone
   - SL beyond zone
   - Target: Previous swing high/low or next liquidity

## 7. Decision Matrix

```
[Check Daily/H4 Candle]
  |
  +-- Bullish Continuation --> Bias: LONG --> H1 Bullish Trend
  |                                           --> Find FVG/OB zone
  |                                           --> Enter LONG
  |
  +-- Bearish Continuation --> Bias: SHORT --> H1 Bearish Trend
  |                                           --> Find FVG/OB zone
  |                                           --> Enter SHORT
  |
  +-- Bullish Reversal -----> Bias: LONG --> Wait for H1 BOS Up
  |                                         --> Find zone after BOS
  |
  +-- Bearish Reversal -----> Bias: SHORT --> Wait for H1 BOS Down
                                             --> Find zone after BOS
```

---

## 8. Practical Checklist

Prije svakog trade-a provjeri:

- [ ] Koji je HTF (Daily/H4) smjer svijece?
- [ ] Je li continuation ili reversal?
- [ ] Koji je moj bias (LONG ili SHORT)?
- [ ] Poklapa li se LTF (H1/M15) struktura s biasom?
- [ ] Jesam li identificirao valid entry zone (FVG/OB/Liquidity)?
