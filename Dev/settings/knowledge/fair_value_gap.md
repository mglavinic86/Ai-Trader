# Fair Value Gap (FVG) - Complete Knowledge

> FVG je neravnoteza u cijeni koju trziste zeli "popuniti".

## Definition

**Fair Value Gap (FVG)** je praznina izmedu dvije svijece nastala kada cijena naglo ode u jednom smjeru, ostavljajuci nepopunjeni prostor (gap) izmedu wick-ova.

FVG nastaje **3-candle patternom** gdje se wick-ovi prve i trece svijece ne preklapaju.

---

## Types of FVG

### 1. Bullish FVG

**Definition:** Nastaje u bullish pokretu kada LOW trece svijece ne dodiruje HIGH prve svijece.

**Structure:**
- Candle 1: Bilo koja (reference je HIGH)
- Candle 2: Snazna bullish displacement svijeca
- Candle 3: Bilo koja (reference je LOW)
- **Gap Zone:** Izmedu Candle 1 HIGH i Candle 3 LOW

**Usage:**
- U bullish trendu koristi se za LONG prilike
- Cijena se cesto vraca da "rebalansira" FVG prije nastavka

### 2. Bearish FVG

**Definition:** Nastaje u bearish pokretu kada HIGH trece svijece ne dodiruje LOW prve svijece.

**Structure:**
- Candle 1: Bilo koja (reference je LOW)
- Candle 2: Snazna bearish displacement svijeca
- Candle 3: Bilo koja (reference je HIGH)
- **Gap Zone:** Izmedu Candle 1 LOW i Candle 3 HIGH

---

## Inverse Fair Value Gap (iFVG)

### Definition

**Inverse FVG (iFVG)** nastaje kada cijena zatvori iznad ili ispod postojeceg FVG-a, uzrokujuci da gap izgubi originalnu ulogu.

### How iFVG Forms

1. Klasicni FVG vec postoji na chartu
2. Cijena probije zonu i tijelo svijece zatvori na DRUGOJ strani FVG-a
3. FVG postaje inverse gap

### Types of iFVG

**Bullish iFVG:**
- Cijena probije bearish FVG prema gore
- Pokazuje da su selleri izgubili kontrolu
- Visoka vjerojatnost da cijena cilja visu likvidnost (PDH, PWH)

**Bearish iFVG:**
- Cijena probije bullish FVG prema dolje
- Pokazuje da su buyeri izgubili kontrolu
- Cijena cesto cilja nize razine likvidnosti (PDL, PWL)

---

## How to Use FVG

### 1. As a Zone of Interest

- Cekaj da se cijena vrati u FVG
- Trazi potvrdu na nizem timeframe-u (CISD ili FVG within FVG)

### 2. As a Target

- Nakon liquidity sweepa, cijena zeli rebalansirati FVG
- FVG moze sluziti kao profit target

### 3. Combined with iFVG

- Kada cijena ude u FVG zonu
- Gledaj formira li se iFVG kao konfirmacija
- Trazi novi FVG nakon iFVG-a za entry point

## Trading FVG - Entry Model

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Identify Higher Timeframe Bias**
   - Check Weekly/Daily/H4 for overall direction
   - Identify key liquidity levels (PDH, PDL, PWH, PWL)

2. **Mark FVG Zones on Higher Timeframe**
   - H4 or H1 FVG zones serve as areas of interest

3. **Wait for Price to Enter Zone**
   - Do NOT enter immediately on first touch
   - Let price enter the zone and show reaction

4. **Get Confirmation on Lower Timeframe**
   - Drop to M15/M5/M3
   - Look for CISD
   - Look for new FVG formation in your direction

5. **Entry**
   - Enter on the new lower timeframe FVG
   - Stop loss below/above the zone
   - Target: Next liquidity level

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Trading Every FVG Without Context

- Charts are full of FVGs on every timeframe
- Only trade FVGs aligned with HTF direction
- Always check bias on higher timeframe first

### Mistake 2: Entering Without Confirmation

- Do NOT enter on first touch
- Wait for: CISD, new FVG on LTF, or clear reaction candle
- Rule: "Reaction + Confirmation = Entry"

---

## Best Practices

1. **Always check higher timeframe context first**
2. **FVG works best when aligned with overall market structure**
3. **Never enter on first touch - wait for confirmation**
4. **Use multi-timeframe analysis (HTF for direction, LTF for entry)**
5. **Combine with liquidity concepts for targets**
6. **Look for confluence (FVG + OB + liquidity sweep)**

---

## Risk-Reward Guidelines

- Typical setups aim for **1:2 to 1:2.5 RR**
- Stop loss: Below/above the FVG zone
- Target: Next liquidity level or opposing FVG

---

## Summary Checklist

- [ ] Higher timeframe bias identified (Weekly/Daily)

- [ ] Price has entered the zone (not just touched)
- [ ] Confirmation received on lower timeframe (CISD/new FVG/iFVG)
- [ ] Clear target identified (liquidity level)
- [ ] Stop loss properly placed
- [ ] Risk-reward ratio minimum 1:2
